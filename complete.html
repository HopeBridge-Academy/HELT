Next Steps
What Happens Now
  • 1
    Payment Verified — Your payment receipt is reviewed by the examination centre, usually within a few hours of submission.
  • 2
    Screen Recording Reviewed — Your session recording is checked for any integrity violations by an authorised examiner.
  • 3
    AI Scores Reviewed — AI-generated pre-scores for Writing and Speaking are reviewed, adjusted if needed, and confirmed by a certified HELT examiner.
  • 4
    Final Scores Compiled — All four section scores are combined into your total result out of 100, and your proficiency level (Elementary / Basic / Intermediate / Advanced) and CEFR band are determined.
  • 5
    Certificate Issued — Within 2 working days of confirmed payment, your official HopeBridge English Language Certificate is emailed to your registered address.
  • 6
    Certificate Contents — Your certificate includes: full name, father's name, ID/Tazkira number, exam date, total score, proficiency level, CEFR band, and a unique verification code. It is suitable for academic, employment, and immigration purposes.
